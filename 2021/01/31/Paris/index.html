Paris?

Paris is bit drunk…

It’s always tipsy.

Paris is…
you dance on the street with bare foot,

and it feels like the only right way.

It makes you sort of have to face that person inside of you.

The most important thing to do in Paris
it’s of course sitting in a cafe.

It’s never for the coffee…

You slow down, and you start noticing.

You notice the Parisians walk in a way
that it seems like that they know where they are going.

I don’t mean just their destination.

I mean…like in general.

They know who they are
and they know what they decide,
what they want,
what they desires.

I know everyone projects Paris as THE city of romance.

It’s cliche,
but I don’t think it can be overrated.

Wherever you go,
I hope you have a bit of Paris with you.
