「Task1 为图表题，插入不了图片，例文详参顾家北」

・Remember:

  • The task is to describe what you see, and then write a report. Don’t give opinions.
  • No conclusion. Instead, Write a summary（the overview）.

Task1 Question types：
Part 1：线形图（Line Graph）—— numbers
Part 2：柱状图（Bar Chart）—— describe
Part 3：饼状图（Pie Chart）—— compare
Part 4：表格（Table）—— changes/trends
Part 5：流程图（Diagram）—— comparing
Part 6：地图题（Map）—— process

・Essay structure-4 paragraphs
1.Introduction
1 sentence: Paraphrase the question（change the key words）
2.Overview++（Or put the overview at the end）++
2 sentences: the main, general things
3.Details（3 sentences）
4.Details（3-4 sentences）
Two paragraphs of details make you organize or‘group’the information better.

-No conclusion！

————————————————————————————————

▶▶Part 1：线形图（Line Graph）——Show numbers changing over a period of time（more than two lines）

-Show numbers changing over a period of time.
You will almost definitely see more than 1（commonly 3-4）lines.
-compare the lines, not describe them separately.

Techniques（Two types of comparison）:
1.Make a very general comparison（summary/overview paragraph）
2.Compare the lines at specific points（‘details’paragraphs）
（Highest points/trends/peaks/stable periods）
Write at least one thing about each line

-No conclusion!

一、线形图数据的描写
线形图的基本特征是，随着一段时间的推移，图里的元素发生升高、降低或不变的趋势。需要掌握基本动词的表达。
英语谚语有云 Less is more，记多易混淆，每类表达我选择记忆几个就 ok
线形图里最基本的特征有升高和降低，可以用 “v+adv” 或 “adj+n” 这两种表达，相互转化：
v+adv
v. 上升：increase, rise, grow
下降：fall, drop, decrease
adv. 显著 / 大幅度：sharply, rapidly, dramatically
持续 / 稳步：gradually, steadily, consistently
轻微 / 小幅：slightly, marginally, minimally

adj+n
adj. 显著 / 大幅度：sharp, rapid, dramatic
持续 / 稳步：gradual, steady, consistent
轻微 / 小幅：slight, marginal, minimal
n. 上升：increase, rise, growth
下降：fall, drop, decrease

另外，还有线形图常出现的波动、保持平稳、到达最高或最低点，表达如下：

波动：fluctuate, experience fluctuation
保持平稳：remain stable, stay constant
到达最高点：peak, reach the highest point
到达最低点：reach the lowest level, hit a low of…

二、描述线形图的句型
分别用 5 种句型描述同一情况：
句型一：X（描述对象）+ 趋势动词 + 副词
The price of textbooks fell sharply in 2019.
句型二：There was a + 形容词 + 趋势名词 + in X（描述对象）
There was a rapid decrease in the price of textbooks in 2019.
句型三：时间 + saw/experienced/ witnessed a + 形容词 + 趋势名词
The year 2019 witnessed a dramatic drop in the price of textbooks.
句型四：A + 形容词 + 趋势名词 + took place/occurred.
A sharp fall in the price of textbooks took place in 2019.
句型五：X（描述对象）+ showed/took +an upward/downward trend.
The price of textbooks showed a downward trend in 2019.

* 每一种句型之间都可以相互转化

三、多点数据的连接方式
只掌握上述五种句型文章结构太过松散。因此，还需要用适合的方式把这些不同的数据串联起来，使句子变长变复杂，更符合英语的习惯。

连接方式一：现在分词作状语：starting at, beginning from，通常可以在描述数据的开头时使用
Starting at 30, X fluctuated around this level in the first decade.①
连接方式二：表示时间的连词：before, after，后面接完整句子或者动词的进行式
X had fluctuated around this level in the first decade before it dropped abruptly to 23 in 1970.
如果把第一个句子也加上的话，就会变成：
Starting at 30, X had fluctuated around this level in the first decade before dropping abruptly to 23 in 1970.（上条里①的合并）
连接方式三：表示并列或转折的连词：but, however, and, in addition
However, the 1970s witnessed a considerable rise③, and then a peak of 60 took place in 1980.④
连接方式四：过去分词作状语：followed by（紧接着…），后面接名词性的短语
However, the 1970s witnessed a considerable rise, and then a peak of 60 took place in 1980, followed by a sharp fall over the next ten-year period.
连接方式五：时间定语从句：在表示时间的词后用 when 引出一个非限制性定语从句
Yet, there had been a stable pattern at 8 until 2000②, when it began to increase slowly.
连接方式六：表示时间的副词：after this, following this period, thereafter
Nonetheless, X is expected to show a downward trend between 2018 and 2022⑤, and after that, it will probably show unnoticeable rise and fall.

四、线形图的写作步骤
观察图里的横轴和纵轴，对表中描述的对象有个简单的了解；如果图的旁边有注解的话，一般是表示图里数字的单位，不能漏掉（看清单位！！！）
看题目有多少个线形图，通常情况，如果题目里不只一张图，那么一般是有几张图写几个主体段；
如果题目里只有一张图，图里不超过三条线的，那就根据有多少条线来分多少段。；
如果一张图里有超过三条线的，最好是按照一定的标准，比如趋势相似、所属类别相同等把几条线合并起来写，这样在描述数据时才不会因为数据太多，跳来跳去，导致混乱。
如果一个图里刚好有三条线的，那么可以根据情况，一条线写一个主体段，总共三个主体段（Task1 里三个主体段是最多的了）（也可把两条重叠线合并为一段）

* 描述每一项（每一条线）的 “重要数据”。
不要覆盖到图里的每一个点、每一个数据！
只写重要数据：开始点、结束点、相交点、大致趋势、转折点、最大最小值。

▶▶Part 2：柱状图（Bar Chart）
-Show numbers changing over a period of time.
It is the same as a line graph（equals to a line graph）
-Bar charts don’t always show time, they often only compare numbers.

Techniques（Your job is to compare the bars）:
1.Make a very general comparison（summary/overview paragraph）
（Don’t give specific numbers）
2.Compare specific numbers（‘details’paragraphs）

-No conclusion!

一、表达倍数、分数的句型
柱状图的基本特征就是，在适当的地方作比较。
别忘了小作文的题目要求是：Summarise the information by selecting and reporting the main features, and make comparisons where relevant.
最后一项是：在有关联的数据之间作比较。
数据之间的比较，指的就是倍数、分数、相等这三种关系。所以，需要掌握各种比较关系的表达，总结三种句型：

句型一：A is X times more than B.（主系表结构）
①The amount of CO2 emitted from cars in 1999 was three times more than that in 2000.
②In 2000, the average amount of time spent on watching TV on a daily basis by adults in US was ten minutes higher than that by children.
句型二：主语 + 谓语 + X times more + 宾语 A + than + 主语 + 谓语 + 宾语 B（主谓宾结构）
①In 1999, cars produced three times more CO2 than (they did) in 2000.
②In 2000, adults in US spent ten minutes more on watching TV every day on average than children (did).
句型三：There be X times more A than B.（There be 结构）
①There was three times more CO2 emitted by cars in 1999 than in 2000.
②In 2000, there were ten minutes more time spent on watching TV every day on average by adults in US than by children.

二、柱状图的分类和写作方法
雅思考试里的柱状图可以分为两大类，不同的分类，写作的思路也不同。

第一类：有时间推移的，通常是横轴上数据表示时间。对于这种类型的柱状图，把它转化为线形图，按照时间的先后顺序来写（一般是从左到右），因为存在时间推移是线形图的首要特征。（关于线形图的具体写作步骤详参 Part1）

第二类：不存在时间推移的，按照同一分类标准，写内部的各种元素，而各元素又按照数值从大到小的顺序写。

▶▶Part 3：饼状图（Pie Chart）—— Always show percentage/number

-It can show numbers, but it always show percentages.
-Even if there are no percentages, we can also say the percentages with words
like‘around’. The full pie is always 100%.
-It can show changes over time（e.g.years）

Techniques（Your job is to compare the percentages and numbers）:
1.Make a very general comparison（summary/overview paragraph）
2.Compare specific figures（‘details’paragraphs）
-Big similarities and differences.

一、“占比” 数据的描写
饼状图的基本特征是，在一个整体里，也就是一个饼里，表示了若干个元素的比例关系，比例的总和是 100%。想准确、地道地描述饼状图，首先需要掌握一些表示 “占比” 动词的表达，这里总结六种描写饼状图的句型：

句型一：大比例或小比例 + 主语 + 谓语，with + 数字
The majority of sales in Bob’s store come from DVDs, with exactly 60%.
句型二：主语 +（表示 “组成” 的动词）+ 数字
DVDs account for three fifths of the total sales.
句型三：单位 of 主语 + which/who… + is + 数字
The percentage of PC games sold is exactly 20%.
句型四：数字 + 主语 + 谓语
Fifteen percent of items sold in the store are CDs.
句型五：There be + 数字 + 主语 + which/who…
There is merely 5% of the total revenue generated by selling posters.
句型六：For + 主题，数字 + 主语 + 谓语
For the sales of posters, only 5% contributes to the turnover（营业额）.

二、饼状图的分类和写法方法
雅思考试里的饼状图可以分为三大类，在拿到题目时就要决定是属于哪一类，因为不同的分类，写作的思路会完全不一样。

第一类：单一个饼图。在雅思考试中，通常是单一饼图 + 其他类型的图（柱状图、表格等）。遇到单一个饼图的话，应该把它独立成一个主体段，段里按照不同元素的占比，从大到小按顺序写。

第二类：有表示时间推移的，题目通常含有不只有一个饼图，通常是一个图表示一个时间节点。对于这种类型的饼状图，其实也是线形图的变种，我们把它转化为线形图，按照时间的先后顺序来写，因为存在时间推移是线形图的首要特征。（关于线形图的具体写作步骤详参 Part1）
来看一个出自《剑 8》的例题（无法插图，详参剑 8 真题）：
The three pie charts below show the changes in annual spending by a particular UK school in 1981, 1991 and 2001.
从以上三幅图可以看出，讲的是一个英国学校在三个年份的不同开销，分别是 1981 年、1991 年、2001 年，对于这样有时间推移的多个饼图，首先把图中占比例最大到最小的元素排序，然后内部再按照时间的先后顺序写。
看下官方给出的满分范文是怎么按照该思路写的：（开头段和结尾段已省去）
In all three years, the greatest expenditure was on staff salaries.（概括句）But while other workers’ salaries saw a fall from 28% in 1981 to only 15% of spending in 2001（第二占比）, teachers’ pay remained the biggest cost, reaching 50% of total spending in 1991 and ending at 45% in 2001.（最大占比）++

Expenditure on resources such as books had increased to 20% by 1991 before decreasing to only 9% by the end of the period.（第三占比）In contrast, the cost of furniture and equipment（第四占比）saw an opposite trend. This cost decreased to only 5% of total expenditure in 1991 but rose dramatically in 2001 when it represented 23% of the school budget.Similarly, the cost of insurance saw a rising trend, growing from only 2% to 8% by 2001.（最小占比）

第三类：没有表示时间推移的，题目中含有不只一个饼图的。这类图有一个共同点：每个饼里的元素都大都是相同的，目的是为了进行图与图之间横向对比。
这类题的写法是：图中各元素按照占比从大到小的顺序写，每种元素进行图与图之间对比着写。

▶▶Part 4：表格（Table）

Can show:
-Any kind of number
-the same information as a line graph, bar chart or pie chart
-Comparisons
-Changes over time（increase, decrease）

Techniques（Your job is to select, describe and compare the key numbers）:
1.Look for main or general features（summary/overview paragraph）
2.Describe specific numbers（‘details’paragraphs）

表格的分类和写作方法
跟柱状图一样，雅思考试里的表格也可分为两大类，不同的分类，写作思路会完全不同。

第一类：单一个饼图有表示时间推移的，通常是表格里的行或竖表示不同的时间。对于这种类型的表格，其实也是线形图的变种，把它转化为线形图，按照时间的先后顺序来写，因为存在时间推移是线形图的首要特征。（关于线形图的具体写作步骤详参 Part1）

第二类：单一个饼图不存在任何时间推移的。遇到这类表格，表格里数据之间的共同特征是：没有任何特征！（是不是感觉我在耍你…）
确实是这样啊！所以要想写得好，我们需要做的是观察、观察、再观察！目的是从无序的数据中找到一些 “有趣的”、“不一样的” 关系，以便有条理、有规律地描述数据，这样文字看起来才不至于太乱。
具体的写法是：选取第一横或第一竖的元素作为线索，按照元素从大到小的顺序介绍数据，在有特殊的数量关系时，比如倍数、分数、相等时，要突出作比较。
核心是：细心观察、再观察，找出 “不一样的” 的数据。

————————————————————————————————

▶▶Two different Charts Questions

-These are questions with 2 different charts, which you can’t compare together.

Techniques
（Your job is to select, describe and compare the key numbers, but you don’t need to compare the 2 charts together - you can describe them separately.）：
1.Look for main feature in each chart（summary/overview paragraph）
2.Describe specific numbers for each chart separately（‘details’paragraphs, para 3-1st chart; para 4-2nd chart）

▶▶Part 5：流程图（Diagram）—— Process diagram

-It shows steps or stages in a process（factory process, man-made process）.

Differences from previous types of questions（line graph, bar chart, pie chart or table）:
-No numbers
-Can’t compare anything
-Can’t describe trends（increase, decrease）
So, what to describe?
-How many steps
-Where the process begins and ends
-each step in the process

Language for process descriptions:
・‘steps’ language:
-At the first stage in the process
-The process begins with
-Secondly, Finally
-The second step involves
-Next, then, after that
-At the following stage
・passive verbs:
-Glass collection-glass is collected.
-Sorting by hand-（Next, the collected）glass is sorted by hand.
-Grinding machine-The glass is passed through the grinding machine.
/ The glass is ground in a machine.
/ The sorted glass is then ground in a machine.

Note: maps can also show processes.
Overview: to count the steps（materials and numbers of steps）.

————————————————————————————————

▶▶Part 6：地图（Map）—— Comparsion diagram

It shows:
・changes to something（e.g. an old design compared to a new design）

  • Map of an island before tourism & Island after construction of tourist facilities
    （‘Before and after’maps）
    -past simple for before and present simple / present perfect for after
    ・or two alternative designs
  • House design for warm climate & House design for cold climate
    （‘Alternative designs’diagrams）
    -Present simple
    ・or even two alternative locations on a map
    -The map shows two possible locations for a new supermarket
    （—‘Alternative locations’map）
    -Present simple

Techniques（Your job is to compare the diagrams）:
-Describe the changes
-Describe things that don’t change
-Describe the differences & similarities

Important:
-Pay attention to the tenses.（Think about the verb tense that you’ll use）
